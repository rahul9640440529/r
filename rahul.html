Jenkins Scheduling Builds

Poll SCM

  1. Go to Jenkins dashboard > Job > Configure.
  2. Enable "Poll SCM" under "Build Triggers".
  3. Enter schedule (e.g., H/15 * * * * for every 15 minutes).

Build Periodically

  1. Go to Jenkins dashboard > Job > Configure.
  2. Enable "Build periodically" under "Build Triggers".
  3. Enter schedule (e.g., H 2 * * 1-5 for 2 AM, Mon-Fri).

Cron Schedule Examples

  • Every 15 minutes: H/15 * * * *
  • Daily at midnight: H H * * *
  • Every weekday at 2 AM: H 2 * * 1-5
